GMC VALUES ENABLING OUR YOUTH

Grade 12 ABM (Accountancy, Business, and Management) students of Pajo Senior High School were given
an invaluable opportunity to visit General Milling Corporation (GMC) for a firsthand look at how
finance and business concepts are applied in the real world.

Through this immersive experience, students gained practical insights into corporate operations,
financial strategies, and business management — deepening their understanding beyond the classroom.

True to its commitment, GMC continues to support youth empowerment by bridging education and
industry, inspiring the next generation of professionals and leaders.
