ROOTED FOR CHANGE: CDO TEAM’S FIRST MANGROVE PLANTING WITH CLENRO

February 27, 2025 | Cebu City

Share
Share
Post

On May 31, 2025, the Cagayan de Oro (CDO) Team embarked on its first
Mangrove Tree Planting activity in Barangay Bonbon, Cadayan De Oro, in
partnership with the City Local Environment and Natural Resources Office
(CLENRO).

With the presence and guidance of CLENRO officials, the team witnessed
the vital role that mangroves play in protecting coastal ecosystems and
promoting environmental sustainability.

Having successfully completed this initiative, the CDO Team remains
committed to engaging in similar activities in support of the local
government&#39;s advocacy for a greener and healthier environment.
This activity was conducted in compliance with PPA Administrative Order
004-2021, which mandates tree planting for the issuance of a Certificate of
Registration, and also form part of our Corporate Social Responsibility
(CSR) efforts.

Together, let’s keep planting the seeds of change—for our communities, our
coasts, and the future we share.
