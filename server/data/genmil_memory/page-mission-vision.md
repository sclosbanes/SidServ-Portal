OUR

<SPAN DATA-METADATA="">SOCIAL RESPONSIBILITIES

GMC is dedicated to creating positive, sustainable impacts in the communities where we operate. Our
commitment to corporate social responsibility (CSR) focuses on environmental protection and
education enhancement. Both management and employees actively participate in initiatives like
mangrove tree planting and coastal clean-ups in Mactan and nearby islands, helping to protect
shorelines, preserve water quality, save marine life, and improve community well-being.

Education is another key focus of GMC’s corporate engagement. In collaboration with Silliman
University’s College of Agriculture, GMC has enhanced Animal Science education by establishing the
SU – G.K. Young Feed Laboratory, equipped with essential lab tools for basic feed testing. GMC also
offers ongoing lab training and animal feed trial experiences to support the hands-on learning of
Animal Science students at Silliman University.
