BAKERY FAIR 2025

This March 6-8, General Milling Corporation was proud to have been a part of the Bakery Fair 2025 at
the World Trade Center in Metro Manila. The event was a whirlwind of excitement and innovation, and
we’re grateful for the experiences we shared!

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS

THE ART AND SCIENCE BEHIND BAKERY INNOVATIONS

Engaging Demonstrations: Our live demos showcased the art and science behind bakery innovations,
offering attendees a firsthand look at the latest baking techniques and technologies.

Product Showcases: We introduced our newest products, which received fantastic feedback from the
crowd. Thank you for the amazing response!

Meaningful Connections: It was a pleasure to have met so many passionate individuals and businesses
who share our love for baking. Networking with other industry leaders and attendees was truly
enriching.

 A Big Thank You We want to extend a heartfelt thank you to everyone who visited us. Your interest
and enthusiasm made this event a great success!

  Memorable Moments: We captured some wonderful moments from the fair, showcasing our team in action
and the vibrant atmosphere of the event. Stay tuned to our page for photos and more!

  Looking Ahead: The insights gained and the connections made at the Bakery Fair 2025 have inspired
us to bring even more innovative solutions to the baking industry. We can’t wait to show you what we
have in store! 

Stay connected with us for updates on future events and all things baking. Together, let’s continue
to innovate and elevate the art of bakery!
