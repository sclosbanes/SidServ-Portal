GENERAL MILLING CORPORATION DEALER'S NIGHT

General Milling Corporation (GMC) proudly hosted its highly anticipated Dealer’s Meet 2025 at the
prestigious Fili Hotel, NUSTAR Cebu, bringing together its trusted partners and valued dealers from
across the country for a day of celebration, collaboration, and strategic alignment.

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS

STRONGER TOGETHER: BUILDING GROWTH THROUGH PARTNERSHIP

With the theme “Stronger Together: Building Growth Through Partnership,” the event highlighted GMC’s
appreciation for its dealers’ unwavering support and significant contributions to the company’s
continued success. The program featured an exciting mix of business updates, inspiring messages from
company executives, recognition of top-performing dealers, and a forward-looking outlook for 2025
and beyond.

Attendees enjoyed a day of meaningful engagement, capped off by a sumptuous dinner, raffle prizes,
entertainment, and networking opportunities in the elegant setting of Fili Hotel. Special awards
were also given to recognize outstanding dealer performance in various categories, showcasing
excellence in sales, customer service, and regional growth.
