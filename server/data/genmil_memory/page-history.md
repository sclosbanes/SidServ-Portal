THE GMC OF

TODAY

As the Company reaches its 60th year of existence and operation, it remains strong, resilient and
focused in providing quality products and services to its customers. In pursuit thereof, it
continues to diversify its operation with its manufacturing complex in Lapu-Lapu City, Cebu, a feed
milling plant and corn buying terminal in Cagayan De Oro, and its grain storage facility in General
Santos City.
