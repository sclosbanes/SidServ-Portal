## Content

AT GENERAL MILLING CORPORATION, WE BELIEVE IN BEING PRESENT—NOT JUST AT THE TABLE, BUT IN LIFE’S
MOST MEANINGFUL MOMENTS.

June 25, 2025

As these young learners take a step forward in their journey, we make sure we’re right there with
them.

Not even the bad weather could stop us last June 25, 2025. We brought joy, laughter, and light to
the Pajo Kindergarten and Grade 3 pupils—armed with school supplies and snacks to brighten their
day.“

Personally, handing out the supplies were Mr. Edwin Y. Kho, GMC-Financial Director; Mrs. Hendrita C.
Debalucos, GMC-HR and Admin Manager; along with the HR/EHS Team; Mr. John Michael Armada, GMC-
VisMin Controller; and representatives from various departments—each one extending support and
sharing in the joy of seeing our children’s smiles.

In addition, GMC has provided paint materials to nearly four schools: Pajo Elementary School, Pajo
National High School, Pajo Senior High School, and Mactan Benito Ebuen Air Base Elementary
School—helping create brighter, more vibrant learning spaces for students.

This meaningful initiative is also part of our Corporate Social Responsibility—reflecting General
Milling Corporation’s unwavering commitment to community.

Because at General Milling Corporation, we do more than provide meals.
We support. We care. We celebrate every milestone—together.
